Privacy

DSA advocates in adhering to the guidelines provided by Personal Data Protection Commission in the way we collect, use and disclose your personal data. Should you have any feedback or enquiries relating to your personal data or how DSA may communicate with you via telephone, or if you wish to stop receiving promotional or marketing messages via your telephone, please contact:

The Personal Data Protection Officer at the following addresses: Email: dpo@downsyndrome-singapore.org Postal Address: 9, Bishan Place #09-01 Junction 8 Office Tower, Singapore 579837 Telephone Number: 6500 9321
