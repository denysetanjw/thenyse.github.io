Do your part for the Down syndrome community

Your Donation,
Changes Lives.

Down Syndrome Association (Singapore) relies on various sources of funding including funding from NCSS (individual project funding on a short-term basis), grants, corporate sponsorships, donations and fundraising activities.

The funds you help us to raise allows us to maintain and expand our services to meet the varying and growing needs of children and adults with Down syndrome and their families.

Like a drop of water that creates thousands of ripples, your kind actions will go a long way.

Donate at your own time and convenience with DBS iBanking

Pledge a sum monthly to make a difference

Donate any amount

Donate at over 870 stations islandwide

Donate once, or monthly.

Please fill up the donor info form to facilitate the tax reduction process

DONATE THROUGH CHEQUE

Kindly issue a crossed cheque payable to "Down Syndrome Association (Singapore)" and mail it to us at DSA(S):

9, Bishan Place, #09-01 Junction 8 Office Tower, Singapore 579837.

Attention to "Team CPCC". Please include your NRIC name/Registered business name and NRIC/FIN/UEN no. for tax deduction purposes.

*All outright donations will be enlisted to a tax deduction of 2.5 times the amount of donation. A Tax Deductible Receipt will be issued for donations above $50.
