Welcome to Down Syndrome
Association Singapore!

Developing individuals, one at a time.

Founded in 1995 to support and educate families, professionals and the general public about Down syndrome, DSA aspires to be the centre of excellence for individuals with Down syndrome, their families and the community.

MAKE A DIFFERENCE

Donate

The funds you help us to raise allows us to maintain and expand our services to meet the needs of individuals with Down syndrome and their families.

Volunteer

Volunteering your time and expertise can be a highly rewarding experience. Join us today and make a difference in the lives of individuals with Down syndrome.
